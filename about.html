OUR MISSION

Hidayat intends to spread a ray of hope and positivity. We hope to create awareness and educate our people on issues prevalent in societies. This includes ways to explore and find successful career paths and analyze global settings.

Moreover, we want to spread accurate information on the causes and prevention methods of diseases like Hepatitis, Malaria, Dengue and HIV/AIDS. In addition, to make our younger generation aware of how they unknowingly are getting harassed and educate them on its prevention.
